# Collectiv — Tester Guide 🃏

Thanks for trying **Collectiv** — an app for collectors to catalog cards, trade
locally, and connect with other collectors. You're testing an early build, so a
few things are intentionally unfinished (noted below). Your feedback is gold. 🙏

**About 15–20 minutes.** Even partial testing helps.

---

## 1. Install & open (5 min)

> ℹ️ The developer will send you a **link/QR code** and may add your email to the
> test list. Use whichever applies to your phone.

### iPhone
1. Install **Expo Go** from the App Store.
2. Open the **link** the developer sent (or scan the **QR code** with your Camera app).
3. It opens in Expo Go. If asked, allow it to load.

### Android
1. Open the **APK download link** the developer sent.
2. Allow "install from this source" if prompted, then install and open **Collectiv**.
   _(Or use Expo Go + the QR code, same as iPhone.)_

If it won't load, screenshot any error and send it back — that itself is useful.

---

## 2. Create your account (2 min)

1. Tap **Create account**.
2. Enter a **display name**, **email**, and a **password** (8+ characters).
3. You'll land on the app automatically. 🎉
4. Close the app fully and reopen it — you should **still be logged in**.

✅ _Check: signup worked, and you stayed logged in after reopening._

---

## 3. Add your first card (5 min) — the core feature

1. Tap the **Portfolio** tab (bottom).
2. Tap **Add your first card** (or the **+** button).
3. Tap the **Front** box → **take a photo of a card**. Line the card up inside the
   frame — it snaps and crops to the card. _(Or "Choose from library".)_ Add a
   **Back** photo too if you like.
4. Tap **Next**, fill in **name** (e.g. "Charizard"), **set**, **condition**, and
   choose **Showcase**.
5. Tap **Save card** → it appears in your collection grid.

✅ _Check: photo captured & cropped okay, card saved with its picture._

**Try also:**
- Tap the card → **Showcase / Unshowcase**, **Edit**, or **Delete** (the **…** menu).
- Make a **new collection**: tap the collection name at the top → **New collection**.
- Tap the **⚙ filter** icon → switch between **Grid** and **List**.

---

## 4. List a card for sale (3 min)

1. Open a card → **List for sale**.
2. Enter a **price** and optional pickup/description → **List card**.
3. Go to the **Market** tab → your listing shows up with its price.
4. Tap it → you'll see the listing detail. As the seller you can **Mark sold** or
   **Cancel** (also from the **receipt icon** → My listings).

> 💡 There are **no payments** in the app — it's just for connecting buyers and
> sellers, like Facebook Marketplace.

✅ _Check: listing appears in Market, and Mark sold removes it._

---

## 5. The social stuff (needs a buddy 👯)

These are best with a **second tester** (a friend, or ask the developer). Have them
make an account and add/showcase a card too.

- **Follow:** open their profile (see step 6) → **Follow**. Your **Home** feed now
  shows what they showcase or list.
- **Message:** on their listing or profile → **Message** → send a message. They
  should get it **instantly** (and can reply live). ⚡
- **Like & comment:** on a listing or showcased card, tap the **heart** and add a
  **comment**. They see it live; tap the like count to see **who liked**.
- **Notifications:** the **bell** on the Home tab shows a badge when someone
  follows / likes / comments. Tap it to see the list.

✅ _Check: messages and likes/comments update in real time on both phones._

---

## 6. Find other collectors

1. Tap the **Social** tab.
2. Type a collector's **@username** in the search box.
3. Tap a result to open their **public profile** (their showcased cards, follow,
   message).

---

## What's intentionally unfinished (don't report these)

- 🚫 **No payments / checkout** — coordination only.
- 👤 **Profile avatars are just initials** (card photos are real).
- 🔑 **"Forgot password" doesn't work yet.**
- 🗺️ **Map, Groups, and Events tabs are placeholders** — coming later.
- 🔔 Notifications are **in-app only** (no push notifications to your lock screen).
